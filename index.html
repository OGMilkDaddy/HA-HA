Welcome to my website! This is my little corner of the internet to showcase my journey in comedy, share highlights, merch, and laughs!

Hi I'm Josh Glass, but many know me as OG Milk Daddy. I started my journey into standup comedy debuting at the virginia beach FunnyBone Clash of the comics January 11th 2024, and yes that went as horribly as you think it would it was my first time after all.

Since that day I have been crafting my skills to become a giggle machine and room killer. Before you ask, yes I am silly goose certified.

When I'm not on stage you can find me crafting new material, making music, building websites, or Overdosing on dungeons and dragons and Magic the gathering.

you can catch me at almost every open mic in the 757 area, I love creating resources for the community I wish were around when I was first starting. I have linked the calendar I made to document all open mics in the 757 area for anyone to try or watch!

My comedy has been described as relatable but ridiculous—and if you’re looking for someone who can bring energy, originality, and a little absurdity to the mic, you’ve found your guy.
