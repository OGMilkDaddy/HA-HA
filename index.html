About

Hi I'm Josh Glass, but many know me as OG Milk Daddy. I started my journey into standup comedy debuting at the virginia beach FunnyBone Clash of the comics January 11th 2024.

My comedy has been described as relatable but ridiculous. If you’re looking for someone who can bring energy, originality, and a little absurdity to the mic, you’ve found your guy.
